Our mission

Directory.Audio is a community collaborative, free database of sound effects, music and audio intros released under a Creative Commons license.

Find the files you need in a few clicks. Filter files by sample rate, bit rate, mood, styles and musical instruments.

Make your contribution. Download, create your artwork and share it with other community users. Share own sound effects, music or intro music.

You can use sound effects, music and intro music downloaded from this site in your videos, YouTube, TikTok and Vimeo content, podcasts, radio shows, movies, games, advertisements and any other products.

The main thing is to abide with the type of license under which the material you need is distributed.

---

What you need to know about the Creative Commons license

CC0
Creative Commons 0
You can use sound effects, music, intro music and other materials distributed under the Creative Commons 0 license in your products in any way, anywhere and without any restrictions.

Attribution
Creative Commons Attribution
You can use sound effects, music, intro music and other materials distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution license anywhere and anywhere.
But you must specify in the description of your product the author of the material used and a direct link to the source.

Non-Commercial
Attribution Non-Commercial
You can use sound effects, music, intro music, and other materials distributed under a Creative Commons Non-Commercial license in NON commercial purposes in any way and anywhere.
You must also specify in the description of your product the author of the material used and a direct link to the source.
