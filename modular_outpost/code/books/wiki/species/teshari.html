A note on the topic of avali

While often confused due to similar appearances. teshari and avali do share an ancient, vastly diverged cultural root. Thousands of years ago, records of ancient nomadic teshari civilizations speak of bright lights and exotic patterns in the sky. Referencing unseen visitors from unknown worlds. skrell records at this time also show contact with sapient trafficking rings often known as "abductors."

To the ancient avali, it was contact with a higher unknown godly power that whisked them away to another world. To Xerquox and Bilflooboxem it was a bet for a six-pack that they could take an entire village of teshari and move it to another planet without getting caught by skrell border patrol. As such, the abducted group did not experience the later "skrell uplifting" historical period, but were also directly separated from their original homeworld, and genetically altered during their abduction. Often resulting in significant cultural friction with modern teshari, and especially the skrell.

Physically, teshari and avali have visible biological differences. Teshari nostrils are located at the peak of their muzzle, while avali's are behind their ears. Avali eyeballs are also not round, and are much larger. Incapable of rotating in their socket. While teshari eyes are round, and allow rotation. Teshari and avali feathers also have structural and cosmetic differences, especially those on their tails. Otherwise they share clearly apparent morphological and genetic heritage.

Often, heated arguments between avali and teshari devolve into cultural or biological purity due to this history. Something long seen by outside scholors as deeply tragic, as neither cultural group had the chance to develoup entirely on their own without outside influences, and both groups have come to believe that they hold some more pure form of that history. It is entirely unknown now what the true cultural legacy of the progenitor teshari would have been without it's destruction by the skrell.
